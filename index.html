Jubilee Sounds

On the morning of October 17, 2014, I woke up with a clear and unmistakable message: "It is time for you to record your music." I knew instantly that this was the voice of God. My response, however, was simple: "What music?" I had no music to record, or so I thought.

Then, I heard the Lord say: "You will record 7 instrumentals, each 7 minutes long, to prepare My people for their Jubilee." The call was clear: I had been commissioned by God to create these seven pieces of music. The urgency and significance of the task overwhelmed me, and it felt as though failure wasn't an option. I sensed that this music was already written in heaven, and my role was simply to receive it and bring it to life.

At that time, I knew little about the concept of Jubilee beyond what I had read in the book of Leviticus. Why would God call me to create music tied to this ancient tradition? Despite my limited understanding, I felt a profound sense of divine purpose in the request. It seemed as though this assignment was connected to something far greater than I could fully grasp at that moment. The urgency of the message only deepened my sense of calling.

From this encounter, two significant things came to pass:

  1. The Creation of the Jubilee Sounds Soundtrack

    I obeyed the call and created a CD featuring 7 instrumental tracks, each 7 minutes long. This project not only fulfilled God's command but also received recognition, being nominated for Instrumental of the Year at the Gospel Music Association (GMA) of Canada.

  2. A Revelation of the Modern-Day Jubilee

    This divine encounter sparked a deeper revelation about the significance of Jubilee in today's world. I came to understand that this call wasn't just about creating music. It was part of a broader, prophetic message leading to the restoration of the earth to its original, God-intended purpose.

The purpose of this website

The purpose of this website is to share the revelations that emerged from this profound encounter with the Lord. Through music, reflection, and understanding, I invite you to join me on this journey of restoration and explore how modern-day Jubilees are unfolding in our time and how we can participate in them.

I can be reached at JubileeSounds@proton.me
